Biography

I am currently a third-year (2022-now) Ph.D. student in the School of Automation of Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU), supervised by Prof. Dingwen Zhang Zhang and Prof. Junwei Han (IEEE Fellow). My research interests lie in Representitve Learning, 3D Gaussian Splatting(3DGS), and 3D AI-Generated Content (AIGC).

I am also joining AIGC Group, StepFun Inc. as Research Intern, led by Xuanyang Zhang and Dr. Gang Yu.

From 2023 to 2024, I worked as a research intern at the Robotcis Team, ByteDance AI Lab, under the mentorship of Minghan Qin. I also interned with the VIS, at Baidu Inc., guided by Dr. Chenming Wu and Jingdong Wang (IEEE Fellow). In 2022 to 2023,I was a research intern at Zhejiang Lab, leading with Prof. Lechao Cheng Cheng .
